As a Tutor, how can I benefit from using Excel11plus.com?

Excel11plus.com is an online tool designed to help you prepare your Students so that they have the best possible chance of gaining a place at their school of choice. With our online testing system, you can:

  • Add as many Students as you like to the system
  • Access to unlimited topic and mock tests on Maths and English
  • Manage writing tasks (essays/creating writing etc) online
  • Assign tests for your Students to complete online
  • Keep track of which tests your Students have completed, pending, or in-progress
  • See results for each test attempted
  • See how well your Students compares to other Students across the region for each test
  • See detailed breakdown of which question types your child finds time consuming, difficult or makes careless mistakes
  • Let your students reattempt the questions they find time consuming or tricky or unable to solve
  • See the performance analysis graphs over the time to track the progress and find weaker areas
