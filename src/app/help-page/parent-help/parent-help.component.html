As a Parent, how can I benefit from Excel11plus.com?

Excel11plus.com is an online tool designed to help you prepare your child so that they have the best possible chance of gaining a place at your school of choice.
With our online testing system, you can:

  • Add up to 2 children
  • Access to unlimited topic and mock tests on Maths and English
  • Assign tests for your child to complete online
  • Keep track of which tests your child has completed, pending or in-progress
  • See results for each test attempted
  • See how well your child compares to other students in your region for each test
  • See detailed breakdown of which question types your child finds time consuming, difficult or makes careless mistakes
  • Let your child reattempt the questions they find time consuming or tricky or unable to solve
  • See the performance analysis graphs over the time to track the progress and find weaker areas
